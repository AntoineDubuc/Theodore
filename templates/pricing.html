Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sales Scout pricing

How do research credits work?

Each company you research consumes one credit. Credits reset monthly and don't roll over. You can upgrade anytime if you need more credits.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What's included in the free trial?

Full access to all features for 14 days with 10 research credits. No credit card required to start.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, ever. You only pay for your monthly or annual subscription.

How does annual billing work?

Annual plans are billed upfront and include a 20% discount. You can switch back to monthly billing anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and process payments securely through Stripe.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes! Enterprise customers can get custom pricing based on their usage needs. Contact our sales team for details.
